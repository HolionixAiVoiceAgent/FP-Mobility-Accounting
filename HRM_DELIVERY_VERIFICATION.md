## 💡 The Automatic Salary Calculation

### How It Works
```
Input:
- Check-in: 09:00
- Check-out: 17:30
- Hourly Rate: €15.00

System Calculation:
Hours = (17:30 - 09:00) = 8.5 hours
Salary = 8.5 × €15.00 = €127.50

Output:
Hours Worked: 8.5h
Daily Salary: €127.50 ✓ (AUTO-CALCULATED!)
```

### Formula
```
Daily Salary = (Check-out Time - Check-in Time) × Hourly Rate
```

### Examples
| Check-in | Check-out | Hours | Rate | Salary |
|----------|-----------|-------|------|--------|
| 09:00 | 17:00 | 8.0 | €15 | €120 |
| 09:00 | 17:30 | 8.5 | €15 | €127.50 |
| 08:00 | 16:00 | 8.0 | €20 | €160 |
| 07:00 | 18:00 | 11.0 | €18 | €198 |
