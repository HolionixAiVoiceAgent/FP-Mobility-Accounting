## 🎯 Your New Superpowers

### 1️⃣ **Universal Search (Cmd+K)**

Instantly find any record in your system.

#### How to Use
1. **Press:** `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows)
2. **Type:** What you're looking for
3. **Navigate:** Use ↑↓ arrows
4. **Select:** Press Enter or click

#### Find Anything
- 🚗 **Vehicles:** "BMW" or "ABC123" (VIN) or "FL-123456" (license plate)
- 👥 **Customers:** "John" or "john@example.com" or "+1-555-1234"
- 💰 **Sales:** "€5000" or "#1234" (sale ID)
- 📋 **Expenses:** "Maintenance" or "€500"

#### Examples

**Finding a vehicle:**
```
Press Cmd+K
Type: BMW
Results: 3 BMWs appear
Press ↓ to select
Press Enter → Shows inventory page with vehicle selected
```

**Finding a customer:**
```
Press Cmd+K
Type: john@
Results: John Smith (john@example.com)
Press Enter → Shows customer details
```

**Finding a recent search:**
```
Press Cmd+K
No typing needed - see "Recent Searches"
Click on previous search → Instant results
```

### 2️⃣ **Keyboard Navigation (Cmd+Key)**

Jump to any page instantly - no menu clicking!

#### Shortcuts

| Keyboard | Goes To |
|----------|---------|
| **Cmd+D** | 🏠 Dashboard |
| **Cmd+I** | 📦 Inventory |
| **Cmd+C** | 👥 Customers |
| **Cmd+B** | 🏦 Bank Integration |
| **Cmd+?** | ❓ Help & All Shortcuts |
| **ESC** | Close any dialog |

#### Examples

```
You're in Customers page, want to check Dashboard:
→ Press Cmd+D
→ Jump to Dashboard instantly (no clicking!)

You're filling out a form and want to search:
→ Press Cmd+K
→ Search popup appears (you stay in form, it's on top)
→ Find what you need
→ Close with ESC and keep filling form
```

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Does Cmd+K work in every page?
**A:** Yes! Works everywhere in the app. Even works while typing in forms (opens search on top).

### Q: Can I use these shortcuts on my phone?
**A:** Not yet (coming soon). For now, use the menu on mobile.

### Q: What if my keyboard shortcut doesn't work?
**A:** 
- Using **Windows?** Try `Ctrl+K` instead of `Cmd+K`
- **Browser blocking it?** Check your browser extensions
- **In a form field?** Make sure you're not typing
- Still stuck? Use the search icon in the toolbar

### Q: Can I customize shortcuts?
**A:** Not yet, but we can customize for you. Send feedback!

### Q: Does search find deleted records?
**A:** No, search only shows active records. Deleted records are archived.

### Q: How far back does search history go?
**A:** Your last 10 searches are saved in browser storage. Cleared when you clear browser cookies.

## 🧾 Cash Advances & Cash Expense Tracking (New)

You can now record cash advances given to employees and track cash expenses they later submit.

How it works
1. Record a cash advance: go to the Expenses page → click "Add Advance" → choose employee (optional), amount and date → Save.
2. When adding an expense paid in cash, select "Cash (Employee Advance)" as Payment Type and optionally select the employee who spent the cash.
3. The Employee Cash Summary shows total advanced vs total spent and remaining per employee.

Where to find it
- Expenses page: Employee Cash Summary card, Add Advance button, and Recorded Advances list.

Reconciliation tips
- If an employee has remaining > 0, they still owe receipts or must return cash.
- Use the Export button on the Cash Summary card to produce a CSV for bookkeeping.
